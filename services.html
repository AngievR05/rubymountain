Construction services

Ruby Mountain Construction & Maintenance offers a complete range of construction and maintenance services for private homes, lodges and commercial projects.

Construction

Turn-key construction of new private homes, lodges and outbuildings, including foundations, structure, roofing and finishes.

Building alterations

Additions, extensions and layout changes that enhance existing buildings while respecting structure and style.

Civil works

Earthworks, foundations, retaining walls, paving and stormwater management solutions.

Project management

Planning, scheduling and coordination of contractors, timelines and budgets from start to finish.

General maintenance

Repairs, timber treatment, painting, waterproofing and ongoing maintenance to protect your property.

Equipment & vehicle rental

Rental of construction equipment and vehicles, with experienced operators where required.

Supply of labour

Skilled construction teams available for short-term and long-term project requirements.

Steel structures & storage units

Design and construction of steel structures, sheds and secure storage units.
